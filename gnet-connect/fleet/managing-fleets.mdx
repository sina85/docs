title: 'Adding Fleet'
description: 'Step-by-step guide to add and manage your vehicles'
---

Welcome to the fleet management guide. Follow these steps to add vehicles to your GNet Connect profile. Please refer to the provided screenshots for assistance.

## Step 1: Access Score Card Details
Navigate to your dashboard and find the score card section. Clicking on the score card will reveal more detailed information about your account.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step1.png"
  alt="Click on the score card to show details"
/>

## Step 2: Navigate to Fleet Section
Click on the "Fleet" tab to access your fleet management settings.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step2.png"
  alt="Navigate to Fleet Section"
/>

## Step 3: Advanced Edit Options
Click on "Advanced Edit Options" to customize your fleet page header text.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step3.png"
  alt="Advanced edit options interface"
/>

## Step 4: Save Header Text
After entering your custom header text, click the "Save" button to confirm changes.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step4.png"
  alt="Save header text changes"
/>

## Step 5: Add New Vehicle
Click on the "Add New Vehicle" button to begin adding a vehicle to your fleet.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step5.png"
  alt="Add new vehicle button"
/>

## Step 6: Enter Vehicle Details
Complete all required vehicle information including:
- Vehicle Type
- Make/Model
- Year
- License Plate
- VIN Number
- Registration Details
- Insurance Information
- Maintenance Schedule

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step6.png"
  alt="Vehicle details form"
/>

## Step 7: Upload Vehicle Photo
Add photos of your vehicle by clicking the "Upload Photo" button.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step7.png"
  alt="Upload vehicle photo interface"
/>

## Step 8: Save Vehicle
Click the "Save Vehicle" button to finalize and add your new vehicle to the fleet.

<img
  src="/images/fleet/step8.png"
  alt="Save vehicle button"
/>

<Note>
Keep your vehicle information current and regularly update maintenance records. You can edit or remove vehicles at any time by returning to the Fleet section.
</Note>
